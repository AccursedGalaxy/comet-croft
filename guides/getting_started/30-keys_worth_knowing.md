# In your inventory

**R** shows how the hovered item is made. **U** shows what it's used for. These work everywhere — including in this book.

The panel beside your inventory lists every item in the pack, with a search box under it. Type a name to find a thing, or **@** plus a mod name — like **@macaw** — to browse one maker's whole range.

Hovering a shulker box shows what's packed inside without placing it.

# Out in the world

**Chopping:** break one log and the whole tree falls — sneak to take just the one. **Mining:** hold **middle mouse** on an ore and the whole vein follows. *Timber and Stone*, in the Building chapter, has the finer points of both.

**Looking around:** a spare mouse button (or **F6**) zooms your view. **M** unfolds the world map, and the minimap always rides in the corner of your screen.

# Where next

Nothing here needs practising — the keys will find your fingers soon enough. Try **R** on the next item that puzzles you. And when your chests begin to overflow, turn to **A Tidy Croft** for drawers and one screen that holds the lot.
