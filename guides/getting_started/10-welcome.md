# Welcome

A croft is a small homestead. This one is yours.

This is a slower, warmer kind of Minecraft. Real seasons turn the year, kitchens actually matter, and the sky over the croft is stranger than it first looks.

If you're new, start with **Your First Days** — a short checklist that carries you through the first evenings. Everything else in this book waits until you need it: one chapter for each part of life out here.

# Using this book

Two keys do most of the teaching in this pack, and they work here in this book and in every inventory screen:

Hover any item and press **R** to see how it's made. Press **U** to see what it's used for.

That means every dish, tool, and block you'll ever meet carries its own instructions with it. When in doubt: hover it, press R. This book is for the things a recipe can't tell you.
