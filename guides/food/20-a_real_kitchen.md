# The working trio

Three blocks turn cooking from a chore into a craft.

The **cutting board** portions and preps ingredients — place one on it and use a knife. The **skillet** sears quick food right over the heat. And the **stove** is the campfire all grown up: flat, tidy, made for pans.

@recipe farmersdelight:stove + farmersdelight:skillet
Set the skillet or the cooking pot on top, and you're a chef.

@recipe farmersdelight:cutting_board
A knife on the board turns one ingredient into portions — cuts of meat, slices, and the occasional bonus.

# The cooking table

Once the pantry grows, remembering what you *can* cook becomes the hard part. This block remembers for you.

@recipe cookingforblockheads:cooking_table
The clever one. Put it beside your chests, counters, and fridge: it lists every recipe you can make *right now* from what's stored in them, and crafts it in one click. The huge dish roster stops being homework.

# Finishing touches

A **sink** pours endless water. An **oven** bakes. A **fridge** keeps ingredients at hand — and stops them aging (more in "Keeping It Fresh").

@item cookingforblockheads:white_fridge | The fridge
Like everything in the kitchen: hover it, press R, build it when you're ready. Then read **Keeping It Fresh** — the fridge is half of that story.
