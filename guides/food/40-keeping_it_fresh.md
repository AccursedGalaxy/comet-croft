# Everything spoils

Food doesn't keep forever here. Over about a week of in-game days it slowly turns, and its tooltip shows how fresh it still is.

It's gentle on purpose — a week is a long time, and nothing rots in your hands mid-adventure. But a proper stockpile wants a plan.

# Where to keep it

The **fridge** stops time: nothing spoils inside it, ever.

**Cabinets, counters, and baskets** slow aging to a quarter speed — a pantry shelf holds food for close to a month.

Pockets and plain chests do nothing at all. So the rhythm is: cook what you'll eat today, shelve what you'll eat this season, freeze the hoard.
