# Variety matters

The same meal every day gets old — literally. Eat one food over and over and it fills you a little less each time. Never below half; this is a nudge, not a punishment.

Mix up your plate and everything stays hearty. With the cookbook this pack carries, the cure is the fun part: there is always a new dish to try.

# Carrying variety

Variety at home is easy. On the road it needs luggage: there are craftable **paper bags**, **lunch boxes**, and **picnic baskets** made exactly for this — pack in several different meals and eat straight from the container.

Search "lunch" in your inventory screen to find them, and R shows the recipes as always.
