# Variety matters

The same meal every day gets old — literally. Eat one food over and over and it fills you a little less each time. Never below half; this is a nudge, not a punishment.

Mix up your plate and everything stays hearty. The cure is the fun part: hover any ingredient and press R, and there is always a new dish to try.

@recipe farmersdelight:fruit_salad
Toss whatever fruit the season gives you into a bowl. Cheap, cheerful, and different from whatever you ate last.

@recipe farmersdelight:egg_sandwich
Bread and eggs you'll have from day one. Sandwiches craft right in your inventory grid — no kitchen needed.

@recipe farmersdelight:apple_pie
When the orchard comes in, bake something proper. A pie sits on the table and serves slices to the whole household.

# Keep it turning

The cooking pot is where variety really opens up — soups, stews, and dinners by the dozen. See **The Cooking Pot** at the start of this chapter, and press R on anything that looks tasty.

One more habit: cook more than you can eat and it will spoil. **Keeping It Fresh**, next in this chapter, shows you where to shelve the surplus.
