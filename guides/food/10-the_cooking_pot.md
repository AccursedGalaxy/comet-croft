@recipe farmersdelight:cooking_pot
Build one early. Simmered meals feed you far better than raw crops, and variety keeps you strong.

# Cooking in the pot

Set the pot over a campfire or another heat source, right-click it, and lay in your ingredients.

Pot recipes don't fit on these pages, and they don't need to: **hover any dish and press R** to see exactly what goes into it. That works right here in this book, and everywhere else.

@item farmersdelight:vegetable_soup | From the pot
Carrot, potato, beetroot and something leafy. Hover it and press R for the full recipe.

@recipe farmersdelight:stuffed_potato
No pot needed for this one, just a crafting grid: a baked potato, cooked beef, and milk.
