# Thirst

Beside your hunger sits a water bar. It drains slowly — a day around the croft barely dents it — but hot places and long roads will.

Any water helps. For a cleaner drink, boil a bottle over a campfire — or hover **purified water** and press R for the other ways of making it.

@recipe homeostatic:leather_flask
Fill it at any pond and sip as you walk. One of the best early crafts in the pack.

@campfire homeostatic:campfire_purified_water_bottle
Set a bottle of water over the fire and wait. A furnace or smoker does the same job, and there's a craftable **water filter** too — hover it and press R.

# Warmth

Deserts scorch, peaks freeze, and getting soaked in winter is as miserable as it sounds. It's a nudge rather than a threat — dress for the trip, keep a fire near in the cold, and dry off under a roof.

Cold weather isn't forever, either — and a warm meal helps. Keeping the garden stocked for one is the next page's business: the seed market.
