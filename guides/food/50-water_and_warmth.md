# Thirst

Beside your hunger sits a water bar. It drains slowly — a day around the croft barely dents it — but hot places and long roads will.

Any water helps, and **purified water** quenches best. Hover the bottle and press R to see the ways of making it.

@recipe homeostatic:leather_flask
Fill it at any pond and sip as you walk. One of the best early crafts in the pack.

# Warmth

Deserts scorch, peaks freeze, and getting soaked in winter is as miserable as it sounds. It's a nudge rather than a threat — dress for the trip, keep a fire near in the cold, and dry off under a roof.

@recipe homeostatic:thermometer
For knowing exactly how the air feels before you blame the weather.
