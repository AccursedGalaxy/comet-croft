# The market

A kitchen is only as good as its garden, and the garden is only as good as its seeds.

@recipe farmingforblockheads:market
Place it and a merchant moves in. They trade emeralds for seeds, saplings, and fertilizer — by far the fastest way to grow the crop collection your cooking will ask for.

# Garden helpers

The market sells the seeds; these keep them growing.

@recipe farmingforblockheads:watering_can
Fill it up and sprinkle the beds to hurry your crops along. The market also sells three grades of fertilizer, each with its own trick — R tells all.
