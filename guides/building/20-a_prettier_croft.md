# The catalogue

This pack carries hundreds of building pieces beyond vanilla: real furniture, proper sloped roofs, window frames, garden fences, lamps and lanterns of every shape.

The trick to browsing it all: in any inventory search box, type **@macaw** to see one maker's whole family at once. The **@** works for every mod name.

# The chisel

One little tool multiplies every block you already have.

@recipe rechiseled:chisel
Take it in hand and try it on a stack of planks or stone: it swaps blocks between dozens of carved variants — beams, panels, brick patterns — so plain walls don't have to stay plain.
