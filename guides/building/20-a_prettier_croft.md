# The catalogue

This pack carries thousands of building pieces beyond vanilla: real furniture, proper sloped roofs, window frames, garden fences, lamps and lanterns of every shape.

The trick to browsing it all: type **@macaw** in the recipe list's search box and one maker's whole family appears at once. The **@** works for every mod name.

@recipe mcwfurnitures:oak_chair
A place to sit is where a house starts. Every wood has its own chairs, tables, desks, drawers and wardrobes — same shapes, your timber.

# Roofs and windows

Flat stair-roofs and glass squares did their best. Proper sloped roofs and framed windows do better.

@recipe mcwroofs:oak_roof + mcwwindows:oak_window
Roofs come in lower, steep, attic and top pieces that meet cleanly at ridges and corners. Shutters swing, and there are blinds and curtain rods besides.

# The chisel

One little tool multiplies every block you already have.

@recipe rechiseled:chisel
Right-click with it to open the chiseling screen: drop in planks or stone and swap the stack between dozens of carved variants — beams, tiles, brick patterns. Left-click a placed block to restyle it where it stands.

# Onward

When the croft looks the way you pictured it, stretch your legs. The **Further Afield** chapter covers waystones, packs and wings — ways to leave home and always find your way back.
