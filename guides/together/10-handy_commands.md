# A few good commands

**/sethome** marks the spot where you stand — you can keep up to five homes. **/home** brings you back to one.

**/tpa (name)** asks to teleport to a friend — they have thirty seconds to accept, then you appear beside them.

**/back** returns you to wherever you just were — including where you died.

**/trigger nv** switches on night vision, for when a cave is too dark to enjoy. Type it again to switch it off.

# Going somewhere new

**/rtp** throws you somewhere wild and far away, for when you feel like starting a long walk home.

When you find a spot worth keeping, set a home there and read on — the next page shows you how to claim the land. And waystones, over in **Further Afield**, will spare you the walk back.
