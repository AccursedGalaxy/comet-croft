# The traveler's backpack

One bag for the long road: extra storage, two tanks for liquids, and a rolled-up sleeping bag.

@item travelersbackpack:standard | Home on your back
Hover it and press R for the recipe — and there's a whole family of fancier packs beyond this one.

@recipe travelersbackpack:red_sleeping_bag
You'll need one of these for the backpack itself. Any wool color works, and a finished bag can be re-dyed.

@recipe travelersbackpack:hose
The tanks won't fill themselves — a hose moves liquids in and out of the pack.

# Sleeping out

The pack rides on your back without taking your armor. When night catches you far from home, set it down and unroll the sleeping bag — morning comes wherever you are.

Once your bag is packed, the road is open. And when walking starts to feel slow, the next page is about earning your wings.
