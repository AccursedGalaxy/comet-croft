# Waystones

Old standing stones wait in villages and wild places. Click one to attune to it — from then on, any waystone can carry you to any other you've visited.

Craft one for the croft, and coming home is a single click from anywhere in the world.

@recipe waystones:deepslate_waystone
Every stone variant works the same, so pick the one that suits the build.

@recipe waystones:warp_scroll
A one-use trip to any waystone you know, from wherever you happen to be standing.
