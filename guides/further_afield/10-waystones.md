# Waystones

Old standing stones wait in villages and out in the wilds. Use one to attune to it — from then on, any waystone can carry you to any other you've visited, for a pinch of experience that grows with the distance.

Craft one for the croft, and home is never more than the nearest stone away.

@recipe waystones:deepslate_waystone
Every stone variant works the same, so pick the one that suits the build.

@recipe waystones:warp_scroll
A one-use trip to any waystone you know, from wherever you happen to be standing — and it asks no experience at all.

@recipe waystones:warp_stone
The reusable cousin. It wears down a little with each trip, but it will carry you home for a long while.
