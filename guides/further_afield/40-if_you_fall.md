# Graves

When you die, your things don't scatter and burn. They wait in a grave right where you fell — safe from other players, with your experience inside too.

Walk back, claim it, carry on. `/back` will even point you the way there.

# Shared ruins

One more mercy for explorers: treasure chests in ruins and dungeons hold separate loot for each player. Your friend opening a chest first takes nothing from you — everyone finds their own.
