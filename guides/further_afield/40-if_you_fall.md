# Graves

When you die, your things don't scatter and burn. They wait in a grave right where you fell — safe from other players, with your experience inside too.

Walk back, claim it, carry on. If the way back is long, **/back** will carry you straight to where you fell.

Before you wander far, it's worth typing **/sethome** at the croft — then **/home** brings you back from anywhere, alive or otherwise.

# Shared ruins

One more mercy for explorers: treasure chests in ruins and dungeons hold separate loot for each player. Your friend opening a chest first takes nothing from you — everyone finds their own.

Which is a good reason to bring a friend along. And to wander with fewer falls in the first place, the next page covers maps, waypoints, and a compass that answers questions.
