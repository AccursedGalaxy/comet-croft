# Maps

The minimap rides in the corner of your screen, and **M** unfolds the full map of everywhere you've been. You can pin waypoints on it, and they'll hang on the horizon in the world until you arrive.

Death leaves its own marker, too — between that and your grave, nothing is ever really lost.

# The explorer's compass

When the map can only show you where you've been, this shows you where to go next. Ask it for any structure — a village, a tower, a temple, a ruin — and it points the way there. The wilds of this pack are unusually full of places worth pointing at.

Use it in hand to pick a structure, then follow the needle. When you find somewhere worth keeping, attune to a nearby waystone and home is never far. That's the world walked — the last chapter, **Better Together**, is about sharing it.
