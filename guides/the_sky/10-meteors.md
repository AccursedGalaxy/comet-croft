# Meteors

This sky drops things. Now and then a meteor streaks down and lands somewhere nearby, going off with a bang and leaving a crater behind.

Finding the crater is worth the walk. The meteoric rock there breaks into chunks you won't find in any mine — mine it without Silk Touch and gather them up. A chunk on its own crafts into a pile of raw iron, or you can save them for better things.

@recipe ohmymeteors:meteoric_alloy
Chunks and iron ingots, mixed. Alloy is what turns a basic laser into an advanced one — hover it and press U to see where it leads.

@recipe ohmymeteors:focusing_lenses
Every laser needs a set of these. Use a spare pair on a placed laser and it will show you the area it watches over.

@recipe ohmymeteors:basic_meteor_laser
For when the falling rocks start to feel less like a gift and more like a problem. Place it near the croft and it will shoot incoming meteors out of the sky before they land. The biggest ones need the advanced laser.

# After the dust settles

Meteors aren't the only thing worth watching for up there. On a clear night, take a spyglass out — the next page is about the stars.
