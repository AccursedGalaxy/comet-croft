# Strange moons

Most nights the moon is just the moon. Once in a while it rises changed — blood-red, pale white, swollen huge, or dark as an eclipse.

Each strange moon bends the night in its own way. Some make the dark more dangerous, some more generous. If the sky looks wrong when the sun goes down, step outside carefully — or watch it from behind a window with something warm.
