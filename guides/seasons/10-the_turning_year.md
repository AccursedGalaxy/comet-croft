# Real seasons

The Croft has a real year. Leaves turn, the rain comes and goes, and in winter the world goes white and the ponds freeze over.

Seasons pass on their own as the days go by — six days each, in Early, Mid and Late stretches. You'll notice it in the grass and the trees first, and the current season sits just under your minimap.

@recipe sereneseasons:calendar
Hang one by the door. It shows the season and the day, so you always know what's coming.

@recipe sereneseasons:season_sensor
For the tinkerers. This block gives off a redstone signal that climbs as its season passes; use it with an empty hand to pick which season it watches. When you're ready to plant something, read on — **Farming with the Seasons** covers what grows when.
